Shadowlands Overview

Quick overview of the changes made to Restoration Shaman for Shadowlands. This article will be updated as Alpha and Beta progresses with the relevant information. You have to pick 1 of the covenants and are stuck with that choice from what we know at this time, meaning you can only use 1 of the ability sets listed below. Each Covenant gives you 1 signature and 1 class specific ability and Soulbinds, which is another Artifact / Azerite like system. As there isn't enough information about Soulbinds yet, they're omitted for now. All of the shaman abilities are hybrid, meaning you can use them to either deal damage, or to heal. Just a few examples of the 6 first known transmog sets in Shadowlands. Some of them are still missing either animations or pieces of armor, and there's different color variations.
